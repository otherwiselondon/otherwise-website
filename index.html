Living Spaces Making Associations

Living

  • The Olive Grove
    An intentional community in Finsbury park, with a diverse mix of musical, physically active, social-movement engaged, curious, creative, action-oriented people who are conscious of how we show up, how we relate, and how we leave our mark on the planet. We’re queer friendly and cultivate an interest in the arts / dance / craft / DIY, and regularly host salons, supper clubs and experiences at the house.
  • Mosaic
    On a mission to build a neighbourhood of friends that live in close proximity to each other and want to build a vibrant, supportive community.

Spaces

  • London Night Cafe
    A small, independent place to do calm things at night. Bring a friend, a date, some work, and/or a good book and settle in for a cozy evening in the stimulating and futuristic space. There is wifi, complimentary tea and coffee (until 11 pm), and lots of comfortable seating.
  • SPACE4
    A physical space for cooperatives or people interested in progressive tech to gather and collaborate.
  • House of Annetta
    A project to repair and imagine the former home of Annetta Pedretti, in Spitalfields - East London. She was a cybernetician and trained architectural designer, and the building became her life-work from 1980 until her death in 2018.

Making

  • London Hackspace
    A non-profit hackerspace in London: a community-run workshop where people come to share tools and knowledge.
  • Community Cycleworks
    Communal bicycle workshop for young people, which gives them a safe space to learn repair skills for themselves, use second hand parts to keep costs low, and an exciting programme of varied cycling activities.

Associations

  • Newspeak House
    Hosts a busy calendar of events at its campus in central London, as well as a one year course for emerging leaders in the field.
  • kin.coop
    Kin is the UK's only non-profit platform for online saving groups.
  • Antiuniversity Now
    A collaborative experiment to challenge institutionalised education, access to learning and the mechanism of knowledge creation and distribution.
